How branching works
1
Fill your picks by dragging players from the left onto your pick slots.
2
Click ⚡ on any filled pick to add a "what if" branch — e.g. "if I take a QB here, follow Plan B instead."
3
Create branches (Plan B, Plan C…) and fill each with a different set of players for that scenario.
4
On draft night, if you time out, the system auto-picks your next queued player from whichever branch is active.
